How submissions work:

Film Church accepts submissions all year long. Every so often when we start programming the next show, we will look at all the films that have come through the portal since the previous show, and all these will be considered for the upcoming program. This means, if you have finished a project and would like to share it even if we are not currently doing an open call, it will be added to the basket of films during the next open call!

Please note that although we do occasionally screen longer pieces, the majority of films we select are under 15 minutes.

**if the link to your film requires a password to access, please put it in the notes section!!
